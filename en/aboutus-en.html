Work is not everything!

Did you know that the average entrepreneur spends around 90,000 to 100,000 hours in the company? That's more than 10 years of pure working time in a row, without sleep, free time or time off. Some entrepreneurs manage even more. However, work not only takes up time, but also places physical and mental demands on people. It also often makes us ill. It is therefore quite logical to think about how we can use these many years sensibly to achieve the necessary personal and financial satisfaction.

Our approach

Based on our personal experience over 25 years of providing advice, support and assistance as tax consultants and partners in a large consultancy firm, the balance between “being an entrepreneur” and private life in an entrepreneurial environment has become particularly important to us. In the course of our activities, we have recognized that the success of a company is not only dependent on turnover and earnings, but also on the personal performance, energy and health of the entrepreneur and their responsibility for themselves. Therefore, our goal is to accompany entrepreneurs on their way to more success, more uniqueness and more personal satisfaction as their sparring partner on their way to a successful and fulfilling (entrepreneurial) life. Communication and contact at eye level as well as a trusting working atmosphere with the client are particularly important to us and form the basis of our mission.

As your sparring partner and advisor, we can help you take the decisive step forward - try it out and let us talk.
