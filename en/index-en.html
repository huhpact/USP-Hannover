What is a USP?

USP stands for “Unique Selling Proposition” and means something like a unique feature. It is what distinguishes a company from others and makes it unique.

The USP describes the unique advantages and characteristics of a company, service, or product. Through a USP, characteristics can be highlighted that set the company, service, or product apart from the competition.

With the Unique Selling Proposition, products and offerings can be established in the market to sustainably strengthen the brand image.

For small and medium-sized enterprises that we support, the USP is crucial for the future success of the company and the entrepreneur.
